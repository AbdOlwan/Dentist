üõ∏ Journey to Becoming a Dentist üõ∏

Step 1: Build Foundation

Complete undergraduate studies with focus on sciences including biology, chemistry, and physics. Develop strong academic foundation and research skills.

Step 2: DAT Examination

Prepare for and excel in the Dental Admission Test (DAT). This comprehensive exam tests your knowledge in natural sciences, perceptual ability, and reading comprehension.

Step 3: Dental School

Attend dental school to earn either a DDS (Doctor of Dental Surgery) or DMD (Doctor of Dental Medicine) degree. First two years focus on classroom learning and lab work.

Step 4: Clinical Experience

Final years of dental school involve hands-on clinical experience, working with real patients under supervision. Learn practical skills and patient management.

Step 5: License & Practice

Pass licensing exams and begin professional practice. Continue education to stay updated with latest technologies and techniques in dentistry.

Step 6: Future Innovation

Embrace emerging technologies, contribute to research, and help shape the future of dental care. Make a lasting impact on oral health worldwide!
